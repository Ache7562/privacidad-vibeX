The data collected is exclusively used to provide health analysis and improve the user experience in the app. Your data is not shared or sold to third parties without your explicit consent. All data usage is intended for educational purposes as part of an academic project.

4. Data Protection

We implement adequate security measures to protect your personal information. We ensure that collected data is not lost, misused, or accessed without authorization.

5. Academic Project

This project will not be published in app stores or used for commercial purposes. It is a research and development project conducted by university students for educational purposes. All data collected is subject to strict privacy policies for this specific purpose.
